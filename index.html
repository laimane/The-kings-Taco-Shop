Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States ?

Tacos first appeared in the United States in the early 20th century, around the 1900s, introduced by Mexican immigrants. One of the earliest mentions was in newspapers from Los Angeles in 1905, describing tacos being sold by street vendors.
